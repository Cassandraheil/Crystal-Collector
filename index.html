Collect the Crystals!


Directions:

  • The four crystals below are each assigned a random number
  • The number is unknown to the crystal until clicked on
  • Clicking the crystals will add that assigned number to your score.
  • The goal is to get your score to reach the random number at the bottom of the screen
  • You loose if your score goes above the goal number
  • The crystal values change each time the game resets
